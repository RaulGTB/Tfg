About Us

RJK Score is a web application born from our passion for eSports and modern web development. It aims to centralize and visualize real-time data from competitive video game tournaments, helping fans access key information that is often scattered across platforms.

Our goal is to offer an engaging and ethical alternative to betting-focused sites by introducing symbolic rewards, personalized content, and interactive features that enhance the fan experience (without monetary transactions).

Built as part of our final academic project using Angular, Spring Boot and the PandaScore API, RJK Score provides live matches, historical stats, team and player profiles, and a favorites section.

Our Vision

We envision an ecosystem where eSports are not just followed, but truly lived. Our goal is to build a committed digital community where users don’t just check scores, but actively engage, personalize their experience, and feel part of something bigger.

This platform is only the beginning. We aim to expand its features, integrate new competitions, and offer valuable tools for both fans and professionals in the competitive gaming scene.
