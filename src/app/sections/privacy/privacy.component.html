Privacy Policy

At RJK eSports, your privacy is our priority. As part of an academic project dedicated to delivering live esports statistics and competitive insights, we are committed to collecting and managing your personal information with care, transparency, and responsibility.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data to provide core features and enhance user experience, including:

  • Account details: username, email address
  • Activity data: favorite teams, saved matches, viewed games
  • Interaction data: navigation patterns, button clicks, session duration
  • Gamification data: reward coins, completed missions, leaderboard stats
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device info (used anonymously for analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

The data you provide allows us to:

  • Customize your dashboard and saved preferences
  • Deliver relevant match content and statistics in real time
  • Track your interaction for achievement and reward systems
  • Monitor platform usage to improve design and performance
  • Ensure secure and stable access to the application

3. Data Protection & Security

We apply a range of technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption, secure access protocols, and strict internal controls. While no system is 100% immune to risk, we strive to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

RJK eSports integrates data from external sources such as the PandaScore API. These services may collect anonymized usage data for analytics purposes. We do not share your personal data with these providers beyond what is technically required to deliver the functionality of the platform.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection laws (such as the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request the correction or deletion of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw your consent to data collection or processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Inactive accounts and anonymous usage data may be periodically deleted or anonymized to ensure long-term privacy.

7. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the platform, academic requirements, or legal obligations. If significant changes are made, users will be notified through an in-app message or via email.

8. Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this privacy policy, feel free to contact our team. As this is an academic prototype, all requests will be handled with educational intent and data will never be used for commercial purposes.
